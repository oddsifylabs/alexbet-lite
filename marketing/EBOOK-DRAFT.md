### Introduction: Why Most Bettors Lose (And What Sharp Bettors Do Differently)

Most sports bettors focus on picks. They chase hot streaks, get frustrated by losses, and ultimately, never know if they actually have an edge. This ebook is for those who are tired of guessing. It's for the disciplined bettors who want to build a process, validate their strategy, and truly understand their performance.

We’ll cover:
- The hidden metric that matters more than win rate: Closing Line Value (CLV)
- How to manage your bankroll like a professional, not a gambler
- Why "betting models" fail without proper tracking and validation
- Building a workflow to transform your betting from random picks to a data-driven process
