# Code of Conduct 📋

## Our Commitment

AlexBET Lite is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and harassment-free experience for all participants, regardless of:
- Age, body size, disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression
- Level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance
- Race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

### Examples of Behavior That Contributes to a Positive Environment

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Helping others learn and grow
- Giving credit where due
- Acknowledging when you don't know something

### Examples of Unacceptable Behavior

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information (doxxing) without explicit permission
- Spam or self-promotion without community benefit
- Advocating for or protecting abusers
- Discrimination based on any protected characteristic
- Threats of violence or violent content

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Community leaders are responsible for:
- Clarifying our standards of acceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate corrective action in response to behavior violations
- Moderating discussion to keep it productive and respectful

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All community spaces (GitHub, Telegram, Discord, forums, email)
- Official project spaces
- Public spaces where individuals represent the project
- Private communications related to project matters

## Reporting Violations

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the community leaders responsible for enforcement at: **conduct@oddsifylabs.com** or **support@oddsifylabs.com**

All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. All reporters and subjects will be treated confidentially.

**Report contents should include:**
- Your contact information
- Description of the incident
- When/where it occurred
- Who was involved
- Any witnesses
- Supporting evidence/links

## Enforcement Guidelines

Community leaders will follow these Community Impact Guidelines in determining consequences:

### 1. Correction
**Impact:** Use of inappropriate language or minor disruptive behavior

**Consequence:** A private written warning with explanation of why behavior was unacceptable. A public apology may be requested.

### 2. Warning
**Impact:** Violation through a single incident or series of actions

**Consequence:** A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with involved parties for a specified period (e.g., 30 days). Violation of terms may result in temporary or permanent ban.

### 3. Temporary Ban
**Impact:** Serious violation or sustained misconduct

**Consequence:** Temporary exclusion from community spaces. Re-engagement may require demonstration of understanding and commitment to following guidelines.

### 4. Permanent Ban
**Impact:** Demonstrating pattern of violation or severe single incident

**Consequence:** Permanent exclusion from all project spaces.

## Appeals

If you believe a decision was made in error, you may appeal within 30 days of the decision by emailing **appeals@alexbet.io** with:
- Your name and reference number
- Why you believe the decision was incorrect
- Any new information to consider
